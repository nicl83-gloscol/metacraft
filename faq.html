Frequently Asked Questions

What version of Minecraft does MetaCraft use?

MetaCraft uses Minecraft 14.4.4, and we always aim to update to the latest version of the game as soon as our plugins are compatible.

MetaCraft Classic uses Minecraft Beta 1.7.3.

Does MetaCraft use plugins?

While MetaCraft does use a small number of plugins for the protection of your base, your items, the server and other players, there are no economy plugins, faction plugins, etc etc.

Is MetaCraft free to play? / Is MetaCraft pay to win?

MetaCraft is free to play and always will be free to play. We do not offer items for sale on either MetaCraft or MetaCraft Classic. MetaCraft Classic is not free to play; however, there is no minimum donation required - everything helps!

Can I be unbanned?

You probably deserved it. If you believe you have been banned in error, or if you wish to have your sentence reduced, please contact us.

How do I report a player that is behaving in an inappropriate manner?

If you believe a player is violating our rules, or if you believe that a player has an offensive skin or username, please contact us immediately.
